Nominal Variables¶

  1. ID

  2. Zip code

Ordiinal Variables

  1. Family
  2. Education

Numeric independent variables

  1. Age- Age of the customer

  2. Experience- Years of experience of customer has

  3. Income- Annula Income of the customer which is in dollars

  4. CCAvg- Average spending on credit cards per month which in dollars

  5. Mortage- Value of House Mortgage

Binary Category variables

  1. Personal Loan- This our target variable which we have to predict.

  2. Security Account- Does the customer have Security Account with bank or not?

  3. Credit Account- Does the customer have a credit card issued by Bank or not?

  4. Online- Does the customer have Online banking facility with bank or not?

  5. CD Account- Does the customer have CD Account with bank or not?
